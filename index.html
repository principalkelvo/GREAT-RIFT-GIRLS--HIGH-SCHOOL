Welcome to the Great Rift Girls' High School

SCHOOL LOCATION
We are Located in Upper Langas Area of Eldoret Town
INCEPTION
The Great Rift Girls’ High School is a new purpose - build Educational institution for Girls Located in Upper Langas Area of Eldoret Town. Founded in 2009, the founders are persons with a long record of dedicated public service and visionary leadership, which they intend to reflect in the culture and ethos of the new institution
GROWTH
The growth has been steady; we have a candidate form(4) this year, and looking forward to be tested.

Why choose us?

Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.

What we do?

We as the Great Rift Girls’ High School we hope to nurture and give to the nation young ladies that will be principled in their lives, strong in leadership, exemplary in service and patriotic in citizenship
