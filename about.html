PROFILE

The Great Rift Girls’ High School is a new purpose - build Educational institution for Girls Located in Upper Langas Area of Eldoret Town. Founded in 2009, the founders are persons with a long record of dedicated public service and visionary leadership, which they intend to reflect in the culture and ethos of the new institution. The Great Rift Girls’ High School is founded on Christian Principles and ideals enunciated by the Late Dr Geoffrey William Griffin, Founder Director of Starehe Boys’ Centre, and other luminaries and proponents of holistic education. .

CORE VALUES

Great Rift High School is located in Rift-Valley province, Uasin-Gishu county, Langas location and Keses Constituency. The geographical location map coordinates for this school is *1.00525 (Latitude), *31.62971 (Longitude).

The Core values that we will nurture in our pupils and practice in the running of the Great Rift Girls’ High School are:

  • Excellence in all our achievements
  • Integrity in all our pursuits.
  • Innovation in all our undertakings
  • Service to the Community and nation
  • Sisterhood in the lasting friendship and mutual support
We hope to nurture and give to the nation young ladies that will be principled in their lives, strong in leadership, exemplary in service and patriotic in citizenship

All those who would like to enquire about the service can come to the school office for enquiries or for list of charges.
All are welcomed
